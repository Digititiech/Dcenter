# Skill: Financial Advisory and Restructuring Consulting

This skill enables the AI agent to discuss and analyze financial strategies relevant to Omani regulatory frameworks.

## Capabilities
* **Asset Restructuring**: Guidelines for optimizing institutional debt and assets.
* **Feasibility Studies**: Framework for preparing project finance models in alignment with Omani banks (e.g., Bank Muscat, ODB).
* **Sovereign Consulting**: Insights into strategic regional economic transitions.
